About

Shiny Windows is owned by me, Brian!

I moved out to Portland in 2019 for the weather, the views and the easy access to the outdoors. I'm an art enthusiast who wanted to be closer to a larger art community.

Before moving to Portland, I operated my own window cleaning business in Montana for fifteen years. Because I am a solo operator and keep my lifestyle simple, it is important to me that you know I often turn down roof work, gutter work and very large homes that would be better suited to a company with a crew. To make up for what I can't do, I make an extra effort to do a great job on the windows I can do.

Give me a call if you think we'd be a good fit!
